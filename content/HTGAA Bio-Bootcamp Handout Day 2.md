# HTGAA Bio-Bootcamp Day 2

## Date: Wednesday, January 21st, 2026 9 AM \- 12 PM EST

This handout is an overview about the topics we will cover on the second day of Bio-Bootcamp and resources that you can explore ahead of or following the Bio-Bootcamp.

## Summary

In the second part of BioBootcamp, you will explore the fundamental tools and methods used in wet lab research. Additionally, we will cover the scientific principles and standards necessary to conduct high-quality, ethical research.
